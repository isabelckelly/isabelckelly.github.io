Introduction

Decades

Decades Magazine is a music magazine that highlights one artist popular in each decade since the 30's. The music taste of this magazine will be Indie, Jazzy, and Rock & Roll. The magazine will contain reviews on these artists/bands, top songs, FAQ's, and best album.
