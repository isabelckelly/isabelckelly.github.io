Introduction

Veridian Salon

Veridian Salon is a new chic salon located in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo. We believe that getting your hair done should be a therapudic and relaxing experience. Hairstyles are something you wear everyday, and we dedicate our time to ensuring you love what you wear. Leave it to us to give you your dream hair. Visit us now!

Target audience

The target audience for this salon would be women of all ages. No matter what age you are, getting hair done is a special experience and should be marketed to any one and everyone of all ages.

This site will be used by young and old people, so it needs to have a clear and easy to understand interface. If someone visits this website, they would be most likely to be coming to read reviews, learn more about the company, learn about the stylists, book an appointment, or contact the company directly. All of these things should be easily accessible from the get-go.

Covid Safety

  • All customers must wear masks inside the salon at all times.

  • If you have shown any symptoms of Covid-19 in the 14 days leading up to your appointment, we will not be able to service you

  • There will be no food allowed in the salon.

  • All of our employees wipe down all equipment with disinfectant between customer appointments.

  • At this moment, we are not accepting any walk-ins. Service will only be by appointment only.

  • Sadly, hugs and handshakes between clients and employees is prohibited, as much as we love them!
